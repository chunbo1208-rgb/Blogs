登鹳雀楼

王之涣 唐诗 五言绝句 zh 2026-03-29

白日依山尽，

黄河入海流。

欲穷千里目，

更上一层楼。

English Translation

The white sun sets behind the mountains;
the Yellow River flows into the sea.
To see a thousand miles farther,
climb one more story of the tower.

注解 · Notes

王之涣（688–742），盛唐著名边塞诗人。此诗仅二十字，却以宏阔之景开篇—— 落日、山脉、大河——最后以一句哲理作结：想看得更远，就要站得更高。

Wang Zhihuan (688–742 AD) wrote this at the Stork Tower (鹳雀楼) in present-day Shanxi Province, looking west over the Yellow River toward the setting sun. The final couplet has transcended its geographical origin to become one of the most quoted maxims in Chinese: ambition and perspective require elevation — literal or otherwise.

The poem's power is its economy. Four lines. Twenty characters. A complete philosophy of aspiration.
